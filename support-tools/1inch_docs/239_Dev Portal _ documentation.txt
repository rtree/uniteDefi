RemainingInvalidator
RemainingInvalidatorLib

The library provides a mechanism to invalidate order based on the remaining amount of the order.

The remaining amount is used as a nonce to invalidate the order. When order is created, the remaining invalidator is 0. When order is filled, the remaining invalidator is the inverse of the remaining amount.
