Introduction

The 1inch Swap API v6.1, Pathfinder, is a cutting-edge discovery and routing algorithm, which offers asset exchanges at the best rates on the market. Pathfinder finds the most efficient paths for a token swap, able to split between different protocols and even different market depths within one protocol in the shortest possible time. Pathfinder advantages include:

Maximized returns. Multiple swap paths are aggregated to ensure optimal trading rates, maximizing value on every transaction.
Enhanced liquidity efficiency. By fragmenting trade volumes and dynamically routing them across multiple pools, Pathfinder optimally utilizes liquidity sources, extracting the best rates from concentrated liquidity.
Expanded market access. Pathfinder is deeply integrated with diverse liquidity pools, enabling strategic distribution of trade volume to capture the most favorable rates.
Optimized execution speed. Smart routing accelerates path discovery and trade execution, reducing latency without compromising efficiency.
Reduced gas costs. By consolidating routes and minimizing redundant transactions, gas fees are significantly reduced, making each swap more cost-efficient.
